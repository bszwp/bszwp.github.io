### 去掉标题上Powered by ECShop的字样

在**includes/lib_main.php**找到lib_main.php文件,打开后,找到156行,将\$page_title = \$GLOBALS\['\_CFG'\]\[’shop_title’\] . ‘ – ‘ . ‘Powered by ECShop’;改为\$page_title = \$GLOBALS\[’\_CFG’][’shop_title’];当然, . ‘ – ‘ . ‘Powered by ECShop’也可以修改为自己喜欢的文字.
